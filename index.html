About ENHYPEN

ENHYPEN is a South Korean boy band formed through the survival show "I-LAND". Their concept blends dark storytelling, futuristic visuals, and layered symbolism. The group explores identity, youth, and transformation, captivating a global audience with each comeback.

DESIRE : UNLEASH

This era showcases ENHYPEN in a cyber-digital universe where desire overrides control. With tracks like "Fatal Trouble" and "XO (Only If You Say Yes)", the group dives into themes of temptation, rebellion, and emotional awakening, set to pulsating beats and electric choreography.
